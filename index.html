CIVICO

Smart Relief. Swift Response.

Civico is a smart disaster response and relief coordination platform that connects citizens, volunteers, NGOs, and government agencies in real time. It provides live mapping of disaster-hit areas, lets people report emergencies instantly, and helps responders deliver aid where it’s needed most — faster and more efficiently.

WHY CHOOSE US?

Track Disaster Zones

See disaster-affected areas in real-time on a color-coded map, check floods, earthquakes, landslides, view shelters and available resources, and get live updates from citizens, volunteers, and authorities.

Report & Request Help

Quickly submit urgent requests for water, food, medical aid, or rescue, with auto-location tagging and photo/video attachments, set urgency levels, and let the community validate your request even when offline.

Volunteer & Coordinate

View and accept nearby tasks, track status and deliveries, follow smart AI-suggested routes for fast response, and earn badges and points while participating in relief missions.
